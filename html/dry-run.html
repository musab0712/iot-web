Dry Run Protection

Dry running occurs when a pump operates without water, leading to overheating and severe damage. Dry run protection safeguards the pump in such scenarios. Sensor-Based Monitoring: Detects the absence of water in the pump chamber or inlet. Immediate Action: Automatically stop the pump to prevent wear and tear. Energy Efficiency: Reduces unnecessary power consumption during dry run situations. By integrating pressure and dry run protection, pumps operate reliably and effi ciently, providing peace of mind and reducing downtime.

  • Sensor-Based Monitoring
  • Immediate Action
  • Energy Efficiency
